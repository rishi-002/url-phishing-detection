1Who We Are

PhishGuard is an academic project by Rishi Kant, a Computer Science student. This is a non-commercial, educational tool hosted for demonstration and learning purposes. There is no company, organization, or commercial entity behind this service.

2What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data — only what is necessary to operate the tool:

Data Type When Collected Stored?
URL submitted for scanning When you click "Scan Now" ✓ Not stored
Email address When you subscribe to newsletter Stored locally
Contact form data (name, email, message) When you submit a support request ✓ Not stored
Session cookie Automatically on page visit Session only
✅ We do not collect your IP address, browser fingerprint, location data, device info, or any personally identifiable information beyond what you voluntarily provide.

3How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used only for:

  • Processing URL scan requests through the ML model (URLs are not logged)
  • Sending you project updates if you subscribed to the newsletter
  • Responding to your support/contact requests via print logs (server-side only)
  • Maintaining your session between page loads (Flask session cookie)
📌 We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating the tool.

4Data Storage

Newsletter email addresses are stored in a plain text file (subscribers.txt) on the server. This file is:

  • Not shared with any third party
  • Not encrypted (emails only — no passwords or sensitive data)
  • Accessible only to the server administrator (Rishi Kant)

To request removal of your email from this list, contact us via the support page.

5Third-Party Services

PhishGuard uses the following third-party resources (all loaded from CDNs):

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Plus Jakarta Sans). Google may log font requests.
  • Bootstrap CDN (jsDelivr) — for layout utilities. jsDelivr may log requests.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, any ad networks, or any other tracking services.

6Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your newsletter email subscription at any time
  • Ask what data we hold about you (we hold very little)
  • Opt out of newsletter communications by contacting us directly

To exercise any of these rights, use the Contact Support page.

7Children's Privacy

PhishGuard is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this service, please contact us immediately.

8Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the project. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please reach out via the Contact Support page or connect on LinkedIn.
