Understanding Phishing & Our Detection System

⚠

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers create fake websites or emails that imitate trusted platforms such as banks, payment services, or social media. These malicious websites try to trick users into entering sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details.

  • Fake login pages
  • Suspicious domain names
  • Requests for sensitive information
  • Impersonation of trusted companies
🛡

How Our System Detects Phishing

Our system uses a machine learning model trained on thousands of legitimate and phishing URLs. When a user submits a URL, the system processes and analyzes the structure of the link to identify patterns commonly used in phishing attacks.

  • URL structure analysis
  • Detection of suspicious keywords
  • Machine learning prediction
  • Classification as Safe or Phishing
