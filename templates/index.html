Understanding Phishing & Detection

⚠️

What is Phishing?

Attackers create fake websites that impersonate trusted brands — banks, payment gateways, social platforms — to steal your credentials and financial data.

  • Fake login pages mimicking real brands
  • Deceptive and lookalike domain names
  • Requests for sensitive information
  • Social engineering via urgency
🛡️

How Our System Detects It

Our ML model, trained on thousands of labeled URLs, dissects the link's structure and character patterns to classify threats in real time.

  • URL structure and entropy analysis
  • Suspicious keyword fingerprinting
  • TF-IDF vectorization pipeline
  • Binary classification: Safe / Phishing
